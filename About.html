What is DanceHall 101really about?

Dancehall 101 is an entertainment website which provides the latest dancehall news our page also provides a music chart and a events page for party lovers.Our aim is to keep both young and old dancehall lovers updated with the latest dancehall scoop. Our news page is to help persons to be up to date with whats going on the dancehall world and the latest and upcoming songs. Our events page is where all the latest events are posted this page is mainly for party lovers as all the latest parties for each month is posted.

Our webpage also allows users to interact with us through emails, when users register they have a chance to get the latest songs and news first, also our users gets to voice their opnion about an artiste or song. With lots of songs releasing monthly Dancehall 101 is the best place to get information on songs and much more.
